## Summary

Tatiane runs a homemade bakery (custom cakes, birthday candies, Brazilian desserts) selling mainly via **WhatsApp and Instagram** in the US (**Zelle / Venmo**). Her biggest pain is **manual quotes in Canva**—slow, no history, no customer list for follow-up. She needs an **internal tool** (owner-only; customers do not log in) to create **branded estimates/invoices quickly**, track **customers and past orders/spend**, manage orders through **Estimate → In Production → Ready**, and **export PDF** to send on WhatsApp. Party orders often combine **multiple products** with fields such as guest count, address, delivery vs pickup, description, quantity, and occasion. **50% upfront** before production is part of her sales process; changes are not allowed within **10 days** of the event. **English and Portuguese (BR)** on **iPhone and desktop**. **Reports** are MVP-priority; full **WhatsApp auto-create client** is desired later—MVP can use PDF download + manual send.

## Synthesis

### Must-have for MVP

- Quick estimate/quote for customers  
- Customizable branded estimate/invoice (replace Canva)  
- Customer list with order history and total spend  
- Multi-line orders (party / multiple products) with mandatory party fields  
- Order statuses: **Estimate → In Production → Ready**  
- Track payment workflow: accepted, 50% received, balance due (manual Zelle/Venmo; no in-app payment processing)  
- PDF export to share on WhatsApp  
- Reports (sales / customers—detail in PRD)  
- Bilingual UI: English + Portuguese (BR)  
- Responsive for iPhone and desktop  

### Nice-to-have (post-MVP)

- Seasonal products  
- Product customization catalog setup (with PM/dev help)  
- WhatsApp integration (auto-create client from message)  
- Production calendar and capacity  
- Staff roles and permissions  
- Notifications, FAQ, maps, accounting integration  

### Out of scope (v1)

- Customer-facing portal or login  
- Online payment gateway (Zelle/Venmo stay manual)  
- Automated deposit collection  
- Internal approval workflows  
- Product photos in catalog  

### Open questions / follow-ups

- Typical orders per week and holiday peaks  
- Region (state) and tax/receipt requirements  
- Exact report metrics Tatiane needs  
- Cancellation/refund policy  
- Branding assets (logo, colors, fonts)  
- Event date / delivery date required on every quote?  

### Assumptions (validate later)

- Single owner account with login for MVP  
- PDF layout is sufficient for “share to WhatsApp”  
- Payment fields are manual status flags, not integrations  

### Risks mentioned

- Losing customers while slow to send quotes (time-to-quote is critical)
